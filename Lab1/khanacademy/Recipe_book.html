Waffle

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
All-purpose flour 2 cups
Milk 1 ¾ cups
Eggs 2
Butter ½ cup
Sugar 2 tbsp
Strawberries 100 g

Step 1: In a large bowl, mix the flour, sugar, and a pinch of salt.

Step 2: In a separate bowl, whisk milk, eggs, and melted butter.

Step 3: Combine the wet and dry ingredients until smooth.

Step 4: Preheat your waffle iron, grease it, and pour in the batter.

Step 5: Cook until golden brown. Serve with syrup, fruit, or whipped cream.

Chocolate Mousse

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Dark chocolate 150 g
Heavy cream 300ml
Sugar 2 tbsp

Step 1: Melt the chocolate in a double boiler until smooth. Let it cool slightly.

Step 2: In a large bowl, whip the heavy cream with sugar until soft peaks form.

Step 3: Gently fold the melted chocolate into the whipped cream until fully combined.

Step 4: Spoon the mixture into serving glasses or bowls.

Step 5: Refrigerate for at least 2 hours to set.

Step 6: Garnish with chocolate shavings, whipped cream, or fresh berries before serving.

Pasta Carbonara

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 200 g
Eggs 2
Parmesan cheese 50 g
Pancetta or bacon 100 g
Garlic 1 clover

Step 1: Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.

Step 2: In a bowl, whisk eggs with Parmesan cheese and black pepper.

Step 3: Fry the pancetta in olive oil until crispy. Add crushed garlic for extra flavor, then discard the garlic.

Step 4: Add the cooked spaghetti to the pancetta, tossing to combine. Remove the pan from heat.

Step 5: Quickly stir in the egg mixture to coat the spaghetti, adding reserved pasta water as needed for a creamy sauce.

Step 6: Serve with extra Parmesan and black pepper on top.
